About the system

How our AI-Powered prediction
system works

Our Plant Growth Condition Prediction system uses state-of-the-art machine learning models to analyze plant images and environmental conditions. Upload a plant image, and our MobileNet-based classifier identifies the plant species. Then, provide environmental data to get accurate growth predictions including height, leaf area, and health scores.

The forecasting module uses LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) neural networks to predict future growth patterns. Get 60-day and 120-day growth forecasts based on current conditions. Our system considers temperature, humidity, soil moisture, pH levels, light intensity, and plant age to deliver precise predictions that help optimize plant care and maximize growth potential.
